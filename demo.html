See how our unique suite of tools makes it easy to record and share interviews, collect photos, and publish a project.

Collect audio stories through our mobile recorder app, available on the iPhone and iPad. Our app allows you to:

  • Bookmark the most important parts of stories.
  • Structure your interview with topic questions.
  • Participate collaboratively with your interviewee.

Organize your interviews into projects. A set of editing tools helps shape your narratives.

  • Sync mobile recordings to the web platform.
  • Refine your interview with the lightweight editor.
  • Easily add locations from an existing list.
  • Add photos, video and text to enhance stories.
