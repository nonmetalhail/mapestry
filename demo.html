See how our unique suite of tools makes it easy to record and share interviews, collect photos, and publish a project.

Collect audio stories through our custom recording device, available on the iPhone and iPad. Our app allows you to:

  • Bookmark the audio.
  • Structure your interview with topic questions.
  • Allows collaborative participation between interviewer and interviewee.
