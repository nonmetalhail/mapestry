Mapestry

A suite of tools for collecting, organizing, and sharing family histories.

Maps and other visuals inspire detailed conversations and rich interactions. Each story is a living document that invites contributions and creates connections from our shared experiences.

Collect your audio stories through our custom recording device, available on the iPhone and iPad.

Mobile recordings are synced to the web platform. This allows you to seamlessly combine and organize your stories into projects. A set of editing tools helps shape your narratives. Add photos, video and text to enhance stories.
