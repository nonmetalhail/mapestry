A suite of tools designed to make the collection and presentation of oral stories easier, Mapestry explores the role of maps and other visuals cues in the gathering, organizing and presenting of stories.


Mapestry aims to reduce the barrier to entry for the individual preservation and sharing of stories and artifacts. View demo

COLLECT

Collect your audio stories through our custom recording device, available on the iPhone and iPad.

ORGANIZE

Through cloud syncing, access your recorded stories through our desktop web platform to edit and organize your stories. You may also download your audio to edit through third party platforms.

SHARE

Create a new project and bring in your family and friends as contributors. Or simply share your finished product when you're done!

Who We Are

We are a group of UC Berkeley School of Information graduate students. Collectively, we have backgrounds in design, front-end development, analytics and project management.
